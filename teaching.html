Taching and Outreach

Please explore my diverse teaching experiences, which have shaped me into a well-rounded educator. Drawing from my background as a Division 1 rower, I have had the privilege of conducting fitness lessons at crossfit camps, specifically focusing on teaching others how to effectively utilize the rowing machine. In addition to my athletic pursuits, I have dedicated years to tutoring, both as a volunteer and through private services, honing my ability to connect with students and support their academic growth. Furthermore, as the academic captain of my rowing team, I have embraced the role of mentor and teacher, providing guidance and instruction to my teammates. Through this webpage, I aim to share my passion for teaching and the invaluable experiences that have shaped me into the educator I am today.
